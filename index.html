Hello There

In this time of COVID-19 finding beds available near our loacality is not viable option, so... What can be done???

So what problems are we facing??


Problem No. 1

Inadequate Beds for COVID-19 patient or lack of awareness about it.

Problem No. 2

No Proper Information or portal to tell the available beds.


So now what??

We implemented a web technology to help people in this crisis.

Point of View

Our SOLUTION.

We created a web application using HERE Maps to track the nearby hospitals around us which will be connected to the hospital's database which will give information about the availability of beds and facilities in that particular hospital.

About Our Solution

It is a progressive WebApp which can be accessed by handheld devices and webpages.

With few clicks find BEDS

Near your Locality

Benefits to people

  1. No need to go to every hospital in search for beds for patients.
  2. All the beds avialble in hospital in real time.
  3. Directly contact with Hospitals to book your bed.
